Saath competes on the one thing nobody else does: making this a job people don't want to leave.

Common questions

Things families always ask us.

How is Saath actually different from agencies I've already tried? +
Three things, concretely. (1) We pay caretakers 22% above market and cover their health insurance, so they actually stay — our average tenure is 18+ months versus an industry average under 4. (2) Caretaker logs, tiffin menus and doctor flags share one dashboard, so a high BP reading on Tuesday actually changes Wednesday's lunch. (3) Our replacement SLA, pricing, and verification dossiers are all written down — not vague claims you discover are false later.
What's your caretaker retention rate, and where can I see it? +
Our 12-month retention is 76%, and average tenure is 18.4 months as of this quarter. We publish these numbers on the first of every quarter at saath.care/promise — alongside our average reply time, replacement SLA hits, and family satisfaction score. Most agencies hide these numbers. We think families deserve to see them.
I'm an NRI — can I pay in USD/AED/GBP and stay updated remotely? +
Yes. Pay with any foreign card on autopay in your home currency. You'll get WhatsApp morning summaries timed to your timezone, plus access to the live family dashboard 24/7. Your dedicated family advocate stays the same person every month — no rotating call center, no re-explaining your parent's situation each time.
What if a caretaker doesn't show up one day? +
Backup on-site within 4 hours in metros, same-day in tier-2 cities. This is a written SLA in your contract — if we miss it, your next month is free. Your family advocate handles the entire swap. You'll know the moment it happens, but you won't have to manage it.
How does pricing work — are there hidden fees? +
One fixed monthly amount, every line itemized: caretaker salary, tiffin subscription, platform fee, taxes. We don't take commission cuts from the caretaker's pay (that's how most agencies make their money — and why their caretakers leave). Cancel anytime with a week's notice. No annual lock-in.
My parent doesn't always speak up if something's wrong. How do I know they're okay? +
This is the gap most platforms miss. Every Saath family gets a dedicated relationship manager who calls your parent — not just you — for a 5-minute check-in once a week. Same person every time, in their own language. If something's off, we hear it from them first, before it becomes a problem you find out about three months later.
Do you serve cities outside the metros? +
We're deliberately deep-not-wide. We currently operate in Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad and Chennai — with strong caretaker density and full SLA coverage. We'll add Kolkata and Ahmedabad next. We'd rather say "not yet in your city" than place someone we can't stand behind.
Can the caretaker speak my parent's regional language? +
Yes — language is one of the first filters we apply, alongside vegetarian/non-vegetarian household preferences and religious practices. We have caretakers fluent in Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Punjabi and English.
